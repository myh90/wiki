Closing the achievement gap model

Click the play arrow at the bottom left or move the "years" slider to see the animation.

Drill and Practice (unfortunately still done in both "traditional" and "Blended Learning" environments) is teaching the students to "skate to where the puck is" - see "reactive performance" graph below.
Since the target performance/expectations are constantly changing, trying to only close the performance gap will miss the target and wident the gap over time! (check off the "reactive gap" checkbox to see the graph).

Enrichment activities have the potential to teach the students to "skate to where the puck is going to be" - see proactive performance.
Anticipating and planning for future skills and performance and being smart about teaching and enrichment, can narrow the performance gap over time (check off "proactive gap").
